How do Journal Submission System work?

A Journal Submission System is a platform or software designed to streamline and simplify the process of submitting scholarly articles or research papers to academic journals. It simplifies and centralizes the entire process, making it more efficient for both authors and journal editors. It helps ensure that papers are correctly formatted and reviewed, leading to high-quality academic publications.
